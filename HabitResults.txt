CURRENT QUESTION: Which users completed the most habits last week?

Generated Query:
SELECT u.user_id, u.user_name, COUNT(DISTINCT hl.habit_id) AS completed_habits
FROM users u
JOIN habits h ON u.user_id = h.user_id
JOIN habit_logs hl ON h.habit_id = hl.habit_id
WHERE hl.is_done = 1 AND hl.log_date >= date('now', '-7 days') AND hl.log_date < date('now')
GROUP BY u.user_id, u.user_name
ORDER BY completed_habits DESC
LIMIT 1;
